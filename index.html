About Libre Space Foundation

Our Vision is an Open and Accessible Outer Space for all.

Our Mission is to promote, advance and develop libre (free and open source) technologies and knowledge for space.

Libre Space Foundation (LSF) is a non-profit foundation registered in Greece from the creators of the SatNOGS project. You can read our goals and mission in our founding statute here.
