About Libre Space Foundation

Our mission is to promote libre (free and open source) technology in space. We support, develop and fund libre space projects and advocate for the usage of related technology in space.

Libre Space Foundation (LSF) is a non-profit foundation registered in Greece from the creators of the SatNOGS project. You can read our goals and mission here.

Projects

LSF currently funds and supports the SatNOGS project. A global network of Satellite ground stations build in an open and affordable way.
